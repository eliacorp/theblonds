THE BLONDS IS A LUXURY APPAREL BRAND BASED IN NEW YORK CITY, CO-FOUNDED BY DESIGNER PHILLIPE BLOND AND CREATIVE DIRECTOR DAVID BLOND IN 2007.

THE BLONDS ARE BEST KNOWN FOR CREATING COLLECTIONS THAT ARE GLAMOROUSLY OVER THE TOP WITH A REBELLIOUS TWIST THAT CATER TO THE ENTERTAINMENT INDUSTRY. INSPIRED BY MUSIC, FILM AND ART, THE BRAND UTILIZES AN ECLECTIC MIX OF TEXTILES AND EMBROIDERY TO CREATE HAND CRAFTED, ONE OF A KIND GARMENTS THAT FOCUS ON ACCENTUATING THE HUMAN FORM.
